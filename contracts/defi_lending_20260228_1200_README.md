# 💰 Lending protocol with dynamic interest rates and credit scoring

> **Decentralized Finance — earn yield, lend, borrow without banks**

Decentralized lending protocol that adjusts interest rates based on borrower creditworthiness. 
* 📈 Dynamic interest rates that reflect market conditions and borrower risk
* 📊 Credit scoring system to evaluate borrower reliability
* 📁 Transparent on-chain loan history and repayment tracking
* 🚫 Secure and trustless lending and borrowing experience
This protocol is ideal for decentralized finance (DeFi) applications, lending platforms, and financial institutions seeking to create a more efficient and transparent lending ecosystem. 
A real-world use case is a peer-to-peer lending platform that utilizes this protocol to provide loans to small business owners, with interest rates determined by their credit history and market conditions.
Built with OpenZeppelin | Auditable | Production Ready
